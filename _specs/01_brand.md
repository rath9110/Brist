# Brist — Brand Identity & Voice

## Core Identity

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Brist (Swedish: "deficiency" — finds what's missing) |
| **Tagline** | "Hitta det du saknar." |
| **Personality** | A knowledgeable friend who understands biochemistry and tells you the truth. Calm, evidence-based, direct. Never salesy, never clinical, never bro-science. |

## Wordmark

- Text: `brist` (lowercase)
- Font: DM Serif Display, 20px
- Color: Primary green `#1F3D2B`
- No icon, no symbol, no container, no background

**Placement rules:**
- Landing page: top-left of content container
- Quiz: absent — no branding during quiz, full focus on questions
- Results page: top-left, subtle reappearance

## Voice & Tone (Swedish Copy)

**Do:**
- Use `du` throughout (informal second person)
- Be direct and warm, not formal
- State facts, explain reasoning, let the user decide
- Use plain Swedish — if a medical term is needed, explain it in parentheses
- Err on the side of understatement: "Troligt otillräckligt" > "du har definitivt brist"

**Don't:**
- No exclamation marks in body copy
- No hype words: "fantastisk", "revolutionerande", "unik"
- No dark patterns: no fake urgency, no countdown timers
- No gamification scores ("your health score is 73!")
- No emojis in the UI
- No social sharing prompts
- No illustrations, mascots, or stock imagery
- No gradient backgrounds
- No confetti or celebration animations
